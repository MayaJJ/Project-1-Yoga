What is Yoga?

What is Yoga? Yoga is a holistic practice originating from ancient India that integrates physical postures, breath control meditation, and philosophical principles. It encompasses a wide range of techniques and practices designed to promote physical, mental, and spiritual well-being.

Benefits

The Benefits of Yoga

Yoga improves physical fitness, flexibility, and balance while promoting relaxation and reducing stress. It enhances mental clarity, emotional balance, and energy levels. Yoga cultivates a mind-body connection, increases flexibility and joint mobility, and contributes to overall well-being and happiness.
